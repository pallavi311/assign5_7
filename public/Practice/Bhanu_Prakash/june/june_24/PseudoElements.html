News Items 1

Confusion and chaos prevailed at Sree Kanteerava Stadium here on Saturday as the state government began preparations for the proposed Covid Care Centre (CCC) at the indoor facility on the premises. The exercise started in the morning with assembling about 300 cots and placing a protective layer over the wooden flooring. But by evening, disgruntled workers were seen dismantling and moving the equipment to the Koramangala indoor stadium.
